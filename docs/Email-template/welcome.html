Before your appointment

Please reply with whatever details you already have so we can prepare for the consultation. It is okay if some items are still undecided.

Land information: Let us know whether you already own the land, are reviewing a block, or are still searching.

Project priorities: Share what matters most to you, such as layout, finishes, number of rooms, outdoor space, or energy efficiency.

Build type: Tell us if this is a new home, duplex, knockdown rebuild, extension, renovation, or another build type.

Location or address: Provide the suburb, lot details, or full site address if you already have it.

Timeline: Share when you would like to start design, approvals, construction, or move in.

Existing documents and ideas: Send any plans, surveys, council notes, inspiration images, sketches, or design ideas you already have.
