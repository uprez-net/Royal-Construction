# Offer Storybook

Five comic-style pages explaining the Royal Construction lead-to-project process with one recurring builder/foreman guide character.

## Pages

1. ![Lead Intake](./01-lead-intake.png)
   - New enquiry arrives by phone, email, or website.
   - CRM captures client, location, budget, build type, source, and notes.
   - Calls, emails, and referrals are logged as activity.
   - The lead moves from New to Contacted with a visible next follow-up.

2. ![Qualified Lead](./02-qualified-lead.png)
   - Team reviews the client brief, site address, budget range, and build type.
   - Consultation is booked and calendar context is sent.
   - Plans, photos, scope notes, and consultation notes are collected.
   - The lead package is ready for offer creation.

3. ![Build The Offer](./03-build-the-offer.png)
   - Lead discussion and uploaded files feed the offer workspace.
   - Internal A-O cost schedule is built for the estimator.
   - SAMPLE QUOTE trade names are crosswalked into the v2 Offer template structure.
   - Pricing build-up keeps internal margin private while producing contract value.

4. ![Offer Negotiation](./04-offer-negotiation.png)
   - The completed offer is reviewed before client discussion.
   - Client and builder negotiate scope, exclusions, upgrades, and price options.
   - Clean offer versions are created during negotiation.
   - The final negotiated offer is marked ready for Tender.

5. ![Tender & Contract](./05-tender-contract.png)
   - Final Tender is sent from the agreed offer.
   - Customer signs the Tender acceptance.
   - Signed Tender becomes the source for the formal contract package.
   - Initial deposit and project handoff start the project.

## Visual Direction

- Premium modern comic style.
- Masculine, grounded construction-business tone.
- Consistent foreman guide character across pages.
- Minimal labels and short UI copy only.
- No customer exposure of internal schedule or private margin.
- Negotiation happens before the Tender is sent.
- Contract package is created after the signed Tender acceptance.
